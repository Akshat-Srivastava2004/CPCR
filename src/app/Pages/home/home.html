ZynoHack is a unified platform where colleges can host hackathons and students from across the country can register, collaborate, and showcase their talents. From registration to recognition — we simplify it all.

For Colleges

Register your institution, organize national-level hackathons, and track student participation with ease.

For Students

Join hackathons from leading colleges, build teams, and challenge yourself to solve real-world problems.

For Everyone

Foster innovation, build your network, earn certifications, and create impact in the tech community.
